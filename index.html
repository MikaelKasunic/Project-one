Why Crossfit?

It will improve your physical strength and help you improve aerobic fitness. It will also improve agility, balance, and flexibility. You will burn alot of calories and manage weight, improved joint health is also a fact

Can anyone join?

Anyone can join, there is no requierments what so ever, all we do will be adjusted for your current physical status. Everyone will get the same benefits regardless of your shape, age, sex or previous physical activities
